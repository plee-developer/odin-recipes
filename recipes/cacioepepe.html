Cacio e Pepe

Description

A simple but complex pasta dish with cheese and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pecorino Romano Cheese
  • Parmigiano Reggiano Cheese
  • Black Pepper
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Create cheese sauce.
  3. Toss sauce with pasta and serve.
  4. Top with extra black pepper.
