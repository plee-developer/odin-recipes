Aglio e Olio

Description

Aglio e Olio is a simple pasta dish with an emphasis on the flavor of garlic and olive oil!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • High Quality Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Cook sliced garlic and olive oil until garlic starts to brown.
  3. Transfer pasta to olive oil & garlic sauce.
  4. Toss and serve.
