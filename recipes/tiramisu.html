Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert. If you love coffee, this might just be your favorite!

Ingredients

  • Marscapone Cream
  • Lady Fingers
  • Espresso
  • Cocoa Powder

Steps

  1. Set down a layer of lady fingers.
  2. Soak with espresso.
  3. Cover lady fingers with a layer of marscapone cream.
  4. Repeat steps 1 to 3.
  5. Top with a generous portion of cocoa powder.
